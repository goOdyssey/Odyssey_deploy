Quick answers 💡
Before you write — your answer might already be here!
How do I reset my password? ⌄
Go to the login page and click "Forgot password". Enter your email and we'll send you a reset link within a few minutes. Check your spam folder if it doesn't arrive!
Can I get a refund for a course? ⌄
Yes! We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid courses, no questions asked. Just contact our support team with your order details and we'll process it right away.
How do I become an instructor on Odyssey? ⌄
Click "Start teaching" on the homepage, complete your instructor profile, and submit your first course for review. Our team reviews all submissions within 5 business days.
Do courses work offline? ⌄
Yes! Download any course content through our mobile app for offline access. This is great for learners with slow or limited internet connections — a key part of our global mission.
Is Odyssey free to use? ⌄
Browsing and creating a student account is completely free. Individual courses are priced by their instructors — many are free, and paid courses are affordable with local currency pricing.
