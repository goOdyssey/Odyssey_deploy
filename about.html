"Resilience became the first brick in the platform I wanted to build."Nima Khoei, Founder of Odyssey

From a setback to a mission for educational equality

Odyssey was born from a moment when its founder, Nima Khoei, experienced how fragile a student's academic path can become. A serious ankle injury forced him away from school during an important period of preparation, while his peers continued studying and moving forward.

The setback was painful, but the deeper wound was the fear of falling behind simply because learning still depended too much on physical access to classrooms, school buildings, and local resources. In that period, a single idea became impossible to ignore: students should not lose opportunity because life places a barrier between them and education.

That experience transformed frustration into purpose. Nima began imagining a platform where high-quality courses could reach students wherever they were, whether they were recovering from injury, living far from strong educational centers, facing financial pressure, or preparing for demanding exams without enough support.

Odyssey carries that promise forward. It is built to make learning more accessible, more flexible, and more humane, so that students can keep pursuing their ambitions even when their circumstances become difficult.

"The advent of Odyssey will revolutionize education and eliminate the barriers of knowledge."A promise for students everywhere
Our mission

Opportunity should survive hardship.

Odyssey exists to remove the barriers that stand between determined students and the education they need. Our goal is to make excellent exam preparation, academic courses, and skill-based learning accessible at reasonable prices, with the flexibility students need when life becomes unpredictable.

We are especially inspired by students who cannot easily reach elite classrooms: students outside major cities, students recovering from illness or injury, students with limited income, and students preparing for demanding exams without proper support.

Our mission is to help them keep moving, even when the path becomes difficult.

💡 What we believe

Our values guide everything we build

🌍
Radical accessibility
Every feature we build asks: does this work for a student on 3G in rural Kenya? If not, we go back to the drawing board.
🎨
Local expertise wins
A maths teacher in Lagos knows more about JAMB prep than anyone in Silicon Valley. We build tools that let local experts shine.
🤝
Fair rewards for instructors
Instructors set their own prices, earn in their local currency, and keep the lion's share of every sale. Teaching should pay well.
🔒
Privacy first
We never sell student data. We never show you ads. Our business model aligns with your learning, not your attention.
⚡
Speed over perfection
We ship fast, learn from real users, and iterate. A working product in students' hands beats a perfect one in a lab.
🧡
Joy in learning
Education doesn't have to be dry or intimidating. We design for curiosity, celebration, and the thrill of understanding something new.
