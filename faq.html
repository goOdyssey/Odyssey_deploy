💡 Help Centre

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Odyssey — for students, instructors, and institutions.

🔍
🎒 For Students
Is Odyssey free to use? ⌄
Creating a student account and browsing all 840,000+ courses is completely free. Individual courses are priced by their instructors — many are offered at no cost, and paid courses use local-currency pricing to stay affordable wherever you are.
How do I enrol in a course? ⌄
Click any course card in the catalogue, then click "Enrol now." For free courses, access is instant. For paid courses, you'll be taken to our secure checkout — we accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, and local payment methods in 80+ countries.
Can I download courses for offline viewing? ⌄
Yes! Our mobile app (iOS and Android) lets you download any course for offline access. This is especially important for learners in areas with limited connectivity — we compress videos for low-bandwidth download too.
How do I get my certificate after completing a course? ⌄
Once you complete all lessons and pass the final assessment (where required), a verified digital certificate is automatically issued to your profile. You can download it as a PDF, share it on LinkedIn, or have the institution verify it directly via our Accreditations portal.
Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied? ⌄
Absolutely. We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid courses, no questions asked. Contact our support team with your order details and we'll process the refund within 5 business days. See our full Refund Policy.
How do I reset my password? ⌄
Go to the login page and click "Forgot password." Enter your email address and we'll send a reset link within a few minutes. If it doesn't arrive, check your spam folder or contact support.
Are courses available in my language? ⌄
Odyssey hosts courses in over 60 languages, including Arabic, Hindi, Swahili, Mandarin, Persian (Farsi), Portuguese, Spanish, French, German, Japanese, and many more. Use the Language filter in the catalogue to find courses in your language.
What devices can I use Odyssey on? ⌄
Odyssey works on any modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge) on desktop, tablet, or mobile. Our native app for iOS and Android offers the full experience including offline downloads, push notifications, and live class support.
🧑‍🏫 For Instructors
How do I become an instructor on Odyssey? ⌄
Click "Start teaching" on the homepage or apply here. Complete your instructor profile, verify your identity, and submit your first course for review. Our team typically reviews submissions within 5 business days and provides feedback if changes are needed.
How much do instructors earn on Odyssey? ⌄
Instructors keep 70% of the net revenue from every course sale. You set your own price, and earnings are paid monthly in your local currency via bank transfer, PayPal, or Stripe. See our full Earnings & Payouts page for details.
What quality standards do courses need to meet? ⌄
Courses must have clear learning objectives, well-structured content, and at minimum 360p video quality. We do not require professional studio production — authenticity and subject expertise matter far more. Our team provides a full quality checklist when you apply.
Can I teach in my own language? ⌄
Yes, absolutely. You can create courses in any language. Odyssey actively encourages courses in local and regional languages — a Yoruba-language JAMB prep course, for example, reaches students that English-only content can never serve.
Do I need to provide certificates to students? ⌄
Odyssey automatically generates verified certificates for students who complete your course. You can also apply to have your courses recognised by our academic accreditation partners. See the Accreditations page for details.
Can I offer live classes? ⌄
Yes. Instructors can schedule live class sessions directly through the Odyssey platform, with integrated video conferencing, screen sharing, interactive whiteboards, and recorded replays for students who can't attend live.
🏫 For Institutions
Can schools and universities use Odyssey? ⌄
Yes. We offer institutional white-label plans for schools, universities, tutoring centres, and government agencies. Your institution can host its own branded catalogue within the Odyssey platform, with custom domains, bulk enrolment, and analytics dashboards. Contact us to discuss.
Does Odyssey comply with GDPR and student data laws? ⌄
Yes. Odyssey is fully GDPR-compliant for European users, COPPA-compliant for learners under 13, and adheres to local data protection laws in all 190+ countries we operate in. We never sell student data or share it with advertisers. See our Privacy Policy.
Are certificates from Odyssey recognised by universities? ⌄
Many Odyssey courses are taught by affiliated university faculty and carry institutional recognition. For formal accreditation, see our Accreditations page which lists partner institutions, exam boards, and professional bodies that recognise Odyssey completion certificates.
What is the refund policy? ⌄
You may request a full refund within 14 days of enrollment, provided you have completed less than 20% of the course content. After 14 days (but within 30 days), if you have completed less than 50% of the course, you will receive a 60% refund of the amount paid. After 30 days or beyond 50% completion, no refund is issued.
What happens if I fail the certificate exam? ⌄
If you score below the instructor's passing threshold, you will not qualify for the certificate. You cannot retake the exam in isolation — you must re-enroll in the full course by paying 30% of the original course price.
Can I ask the instructor questions directly? ⌄
Some instructors offer a direct Q&A service, either for free or at an additional fee set entirely by them. Odyssey takes no commission on Q&A fees.
Is the certificate included in the course price? ⌄
Yes — always. If a course offers a certificate, the cost must be included in the enrollment fee. Instructors are never permitted to charge extra for the certificate.
