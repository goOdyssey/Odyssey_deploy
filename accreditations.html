Our certificate commitment

Every instructor on Odyssey must provide a verified completion certificate for students who finish a course. Certificates are issued by Odyssey after completion and include the student name, course title, instructor, completion date, and a unique verification code.

✅ All Odyssey certificates are: digitally signed, tamper-proof, permanently hosted, downloadable as PDF, shareable on professional profiles, and verifiable by third parties in seconds.

How instructors apply for accreditation

Instructors with an existing Odyssey course can apply for formal review. The process includes a syllabus submission, learning-outcome map, expert peer review, requested revisions, and final approval with an accreditation badge.

To begin the application, visit your Instructor Dashboard or contact our academic partnerships team.

Verifying a certificate

Employers, universities, and institutions can verify any Odyssey certificate by visiting verify.odyssey.learn and entering the 12-digit code printed on the certificate. Verification shows the student, course, completion date, instructor, and accreditation status in real time.
