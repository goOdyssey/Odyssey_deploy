Refund Eligibility (Students)

  • Request must be made within14 calendar daysof the enrollment date.
  • Student must have completedless than 20%of the total course curriculum (lessons, videos, and assessments combined).
  • If both conditions are met, a full refund is processed within5 business daysto the original payment method.
  • Courses that have beenfully downloaded for offline usewithin 24 hours of purchase are not eligible for refunds.
  • Certificates that have already been issued are non-refundable once downloaded.

Non-Refundable Cases

  • More than 14 days have passed since enrollment.
  • More than 20% of course content has been completed.
  • The course is part of a bundle where another course in the bundle has been completed.
  • The student has already downloaded the completion certificate.

Impact on Instructor Earnings

When a refund is approved, the corresponding 70% instructor earnings for that transaction are reversed from the instructor's next payout. Fraudulent refund requests are investigated and disputed refunds do not automatically result in clawback.

How to Request a Refund

Students may submit a refund request through theiraccount dashboard → My Courses → Request Refund, or by contactingsupport@odyssey.learn. Refunds are typically processed within 5 business days.

🏅 Certificate provision is optional for instructors.However, if you choose to offer a certificate, you must follow all rules outlined below. Certificates may never be charged as an add-on — the cost must be included in the course price.

For Instructors — Certificate Rules

  • Offering a completion certificate is entirely the instructor's choice. It is not mandatory on Odyssey.
  • If a certificate is offered, it must be included in the enrollment price —no separate certificate fee is allowed.
  • Certificate-bearing coursesmust include at least two formal assessments: a midterm examination and a final examination.
  • The instructor must define aminimum passing score(e.g. 60%, 70%, etc.) below which students are not eligible for the certificate.
  • Students who do not meet the passing threshold mustretake the course or the failed assessmentbefore the certificate can be issued. The instructor determines retake policy.
  • Certificates must bear the instructor's name and, where applicable, the affiliated institution's name and seal.
  • Odyssey will digitally countersign all certificates for platform verification purposes.

For Students — Certificate Process

  • Upon completing all course content and passing both required assessments above the instructor's threshold, a certificate is automatically generated and issued to your Odyssey profile.
  • Certificates are digitally signed, tamper-proof, downloadable as PDF, and verifiable atverify.odyssey.learn.
  • Certificates may be shared on LinkedIn, CVs, and job applications directly from your profile.
  • Students who fail assessments are not eligible for a certificate until they re-enroll and meet the threshold — re-enrollment at the instructor's stated price applies.
  • Certificates are issued to the registered individual only and are non-transferable.

Institutional Recognition

Some Odyssey courses carry recognition from partner institutions (universities, professional bodies, and exam boards). These are clearly labelled with an "Accredited" badge on the course page. For the full list of accreditation partners, see ourAccreditations page.
