How our revenue share works

When a student purchases your course, Odyssey keeps 30% of the net sale price to cover payments, hosting, support, and platform infrastructure. You receive the remaining 70% paid monthly in your local currency.

💡 Example: if your course is priced at $50 and sells 100 copies, you earn $3,500. Odyssey keeps $1,500 to operate the platform.

Setting your price

You control your pricing. You can offer courses for free, charge a one-time fee, run promotions, or set special pricing for professional certificates and live cohort courses.

  • Minimum price: $0 (free)
  • Recommended price for 10–40 hour courses: $15–$50
  • Professional certification courses: $50–$200+
  • Live cohort-based courses: custom pricing

When and how you get paid

Earnings are calculated at the end of each month and paid within 15 days. Supported payout methods include bank transfer, PayPal, Stripe where available, and mobile money in selected markets.

  • 🏦 Bank transfer — available in 140+ countries
  • 💳 PayPal — available in 200+ countries
  • 💸 Stripe — for instructors in supported countries
  • 📱 Mobile money — selected African markets

There is a minimum payout threshold of $20. Earnings below this amount roll over to the next month.

Subscription revenue and publishing limits

Odyssey subscriptions are separate from course sale revenue. The Free plan lets instructors keep up to 5 active courses and 20 exams published. Pro is $15/month for solo instructors, with up to 25 active courses, 100 exams, richer student analytics, CSV exports, and priority review signals. Institutional is $40/month for schools and academies, with up to 100 active courses, unlimited exams, team workspace tools, certificate branding, and institution-level analytics.

Subscriptions do not reduce your 70% instructor revenue share on course sales. They unlock publishing capacity, analytics, and operating tools for larger teaching businesses.

Taxes and deductions

Odyssey does not deduct income tax on your behalf. You are responsible for declaring instructor earnings under the laws of your country. Odyssey provides monthly statements and annual summaries, and handles VAT/GST collection where platform rules require it.

Bonuses and accelerators

Top-performing instructors can receive featured placement, newsletter and social-media promotion, an Odyssey Pro Instructor badge, advanced analytics, and priority support.

Refunds and chargebacks

If a student receives a refund within the guarantee window, the related earnings are deducted from your next payout. Fraudulent chargebacks are reviewed carefully and do not automatically affect your standing.
